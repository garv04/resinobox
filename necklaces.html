Jewelry Care Tips

While our anti-tarnish necklaces are designed to maintain their shine, proper care will extend their life and beauty:

  • Remove before swimming or showering
  • Apply perfume and cosmetics before wearing jewelry
  • Put on your jewelry after getting dressed
  • Store in a cool, dry place when not wearing
  • Clean with a soft, dry cloth after wearing
